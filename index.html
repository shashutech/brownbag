What We Do

Carry out preliminary assessment of observed climate location over client location.

Make climate change projections under various global warming scenarios over client location for the near term, mid-term, and long term future.

Prepare detailed vulnerability assessment reports that include climate and socio-economic aspects such as atmospheric conditions, agricultural food production, public health, water resource management, etc.

Assist stakeholders to develop climate change mitigation and adaptation stratergies.
